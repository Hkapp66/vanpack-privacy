CAMPERVAN PACKING CHECKLIST

Effective date: June 16, 2026

Short version: Van Pack does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Who we are

Van Pack ("the app", "we", "us") is a campervan packing checklist app developed by Hermann Kappelhoff. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

2. Data we do not collect

✓ Van Pack does not collect any personal information. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Crash reports sent to external services
  • Any data transmitted over the internet

3. Data stored on your device

Van Pack stores the following data locally on your device only, using your phone's built-in storage:

  • Your checklist progress (which items you have checked off)
  • Your language preference (English or German)

This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to any external server. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

4. Third-party services

Van Pack does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app works fully offline and makes no network requests during normal use.

5. Children's privacy

Van Pack does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app contains no features that require or encourage data entry of any personal information.

6. Changes to this policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
